### Slide 2 — Case Study (Challenge / Idea / Result)
- **challenge**: Study Inspirator was an existing app. But the old design was outdated and not as engaging to the target audience. Further more, we had to adhere to the WCAG-guidlines while making a fun feeling questionare.
- **idea**: The new design would have to be fresh engaging and fun. Animation and good writing is going to bring the experience to live. There's a chat function that will guide students through the discovery journey. We've even designed a new mascot!
- **result**: Well the mascot didn't make the final cut. But all the animation were a big succes! Upcoming students are uncovering their future bachelors every day!
